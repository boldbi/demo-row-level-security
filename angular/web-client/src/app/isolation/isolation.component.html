• National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) isolation
    Workflow

    Analyze power consumption across different sectors and assess its distribution.

    The National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) analyzes power usage across various sectors to identify opportunities for energy conservation and promote sustainable solutions. These sectors are grouped as (residential and traffic) group one and (industrial and commercial) group two. To ensure data security and focused analysis, NREL utilizes isolation filters. These grouped sectors are maintained in different tenants, and isolation filters are applied for filtering data and providing row-level security.
